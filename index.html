    Welcome!

    We're Here to Help

    learn more It can feel daunting for some to embark on a therapy journey, and taking the first step is often the bravest. At Collective Psychology we are mindful of this and will help you to feel relaxed, comfortable and informed when exploring your options for therapy. Our focus is on helping you to reach your desired goals for change and personal development, through a holistic and personalised treatment plan.

    How Can We Help?

    ENVIRONMENT

    Our clinics are specifically designed to enhance your therapeutic experience. We aim to cultivate a peaceful, calming and grounding environment, whilst also prioritising the privacy of our clients. This holistic approach to therapy will facilitate a positive and enriching journey towards your goals for change.

    EXPERIENCE

    We are a team of highly skilled Clinical and Counselling Psychologists who have extensive training and considerable experience within the NHS and private sectors. As doctors of psychology we are registered with the HCPC. Collective Psychology is multidisciplinary and our specialisms include working with children, young people, adults, couples and families, and with a wide range of mental health difficulties.

    EFFECTIVE

    We are exclusively trained in a variety of therapeutic models and approaches, all of which are recognised by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) as being robust and effective. We provide individually tailored treatment based on your identified goals within therapy, and provide outcome measures to help you track progress.
